ABOUT OUR INSTITUTION

About Our Institution

Kendriya Vidyalaya No. 1 Naval Base, Kochi is the school behind this website and the community Ashoka House is a part of. As a Kendriya Vidyalaya, school life brings together academics, sports, co-curricular activities and events throughout the academic year.

Within the school, students are organised into four houses for inter-house activities and competitions. Ashoka House represents Section C, alongside Shivaji, Tagore and Raman Houses.

BEHIND THE WEBSITE

About the Developer

This website was made by Christiano S Koodathinkal, a Class XII student of Kendriya Vidyalaya No. 1 Naval Base, Kochi.

I had always wanted to work on a proper web project of my own, and the Ashoka House website gave me the opportunity to finally build one around something real.

The website was created both to organise Ashoka House information in one place and to give me practical experience in web development. A lot of the project came from trying ideas, finding problems, fixing them and learning while building.

Another project I have worked on is the Class XII C Website ↗.

Thank you to everyone who gave me the opportunity and freedom to work on this project. I appreciate being able to build and learn through it. Wishing everyone a great year ahead.
