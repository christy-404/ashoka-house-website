WHO WE ARE

Ashoka House

Ashoka House is one of the four houses of PM SHRI Kendriya Vidyalaya No. 1 Naval Base, Kochi.

Throughout the academic year, students from Classes VI to XII participate in academic, cultural, literary and sporting activities while working together as one house. Every competition provides an opportunity to learn, contribute and represent Ashoka House with responsibility and teamwork.

ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

Designed for Ashoka House.

This website was created as a student initiative to provide a single place for Ashoka House information.

It brings together events, standings, leadership details, photographs and other house updates in an organised and accessible format for students, teachers and parents.

More information about the project and its development is available on the About page.

ACADEMIC SESSION 2026–27

A New Academic Year.

Each academic session begins with new leadership, new activities and new opportunities for participation.

Throughout the year, students represent Ashoka House across academics, cultural programmes, sports and inter-house competitions while contributing to the house in their own way.

The academic calendar continues to grow as new events and updates are added during the session.

Welcome to a new year of ideas, participation and technology. We hope it will be a meaningful and memorable year for everyone.
